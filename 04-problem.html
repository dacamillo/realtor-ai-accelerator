The Real Estate Lead Problem

Every day, real estate professionals lose potential clients due to fragmented systems and delayed responses.

Missed Leads

73% of leads never get contacted because they come in after hours or get lost in multiple systems.

Slow Response

The average response time to web leads is 42 hours. Studies show leads contacted within 5 minutes are 21x more likely to qualify.

Tool Overload

Average agent uses 5-8 different tools that don't talk to each other, creating gaps and inefficiencies.

High Costs

Monthly software costs often exceed $500-800 for disconnected tools that don't provide complete solutions.

No Follow-up

80% of leads require 5+ touchpoints to convert, but most agents give up after 1-2 attempts due to manual processes.

Data Silos

Client information scattered across multiple platforms makes it impossible to track the full customer journey.
